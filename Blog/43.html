10th Vol Concludes The Ancient Magus' Bride: Wizard's Blue Spinoff Manga







A Fresh Perspective on a Beloved World







Published under the licensing rights of Seven Seas, "The Ancient Magus' Bride: Wizard's Blue" has been a delightful addition to the "The Ancient Magus' Bride" universe. This exquisitely illustrated manga introduces readers to the world of Blue, a character who has embarked on a magical journey of self-discovery. The story, set against the enchanting backdrop of Paris, takes readers on a spellbinding adventure through a world where magic and mystery intertwine. The relationship between Blue and Gisele, the skilled magus, forms the heart of this captivating narrative. Through their journey, readers are introduced to a fresh sense of wonder and a perspective that offers a unique and enchanting spin on the original series.

Sanda and Tsukumo began the serialization of "The Ancient Magus' Bride: Wizard's Blue" on the Manga Doa app in April 2019. What makes this spinoff particularly special is the oversight provided by Kore Yamazaki herself, the creator of "The Ancient Magus' Bride." This ensures that the new manga remains true to the essence of the original series while offering a fresh and intriguing perspective. Kore Yamazaki originally launched the core manga, "The Ancient Magus' Bride," in Mag Garden's Monthly Comic Blade magazine back in 2013. However, the magazine was discontinued in September 2014, prompting the manga to find a new home in Monthly Comic Garden, where it has continued to flourish. With a circulation of over 10 million copies, "The Ancient Magus' Bride" has established itself as a beloved and enduring series.

A World Beyond the Manga







The magic of "The Ancient Magus' Bride" has transcended the pages of the manga. The series has been brought to life through animated adaptations, including the "The Ancient Magus' Bride: Those Awaiting a Star" OVA in 2017, the first television anime season in 2017, and the three-episode "The Ancient Magus' Bride â The Boy from the West and the Knight of the Blue Storm" OAD from 2021 to 2022. This has undoubtedly been an exciting time for fans of the series, as they get to experience the enchanting world of "The Ancient Magus' Bride" in animated form.

As we prepare for the release of the tenth and final volume of "The Ancient Magus' Bride: Wizard's Blue," we can't help but reflect on the magical journey we've taken with Blue and Gisele. This enchanting spinoff has not only added depth to the world created by Kore Yamazaki but also offered fans a fresh perspective and a sense of wonder. With the animated adaptations continuing to captivate audiences, "The Ancient Magus' Bride" universe is as vibrant as ever. As we await the concluding chapter of "The Ancient Magus' Bride: Wizard's Blue," one thing is certain - the magic of this world will continue to enchant readers and viewers alike for years to come.
