Muse Asia introduces the English Dub of "SPY×FAMILY" in India, alongside 13 additional Asian countries.







In a recent announcement, Muse Asia disclosed that the English dubbed version of the popular “SPY×FAMILY” anime, adapted from Tatsuya Endō’s renowned manga, will be made available on its YouTube channel for fans across Asia. This streaming initiative will cover India and an additional 13 countries, including Bangladesh, Bhutan, Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, the Philippines, Singapore, Vietnam, and Pakistan.

Muse Asia introduces the English Dub of "SPY×FAMILY" in India, alongside 13 additional Asian countries.






In a recent announcement, Muse Asia disclosed that the English dubbed version of the popular “SPY×FAMILY” anime, adapted from Tatsuya Endō’s renowned manga, will be made available on its YouTube channel for fans across Asia. This streaming initiative will cover India and an additional 13 countries, including Bangladesh, Bhutan, Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, the Philippines, Singapore, Vietnam, and Pakistan.

The anticipated anime series premiered in Japan in April 2022, with its first cours being simultaneously streamed in India on Muse Asia’s YouTube channel and Netflix. The latter platform presented the second cours in October 2022, following the anime’s successful run








Adding to the excitement for fans, a second season of the anime is slated for an October 7 release, while a special film, “Gekijōban SPY×FAMILY Code: White,” boasting an original storyline, is set to captivate audiences on December 22.

While Crunchyroll started its streaming of the English dub in India on February 1, Muse Asia had already introduced it to fans on January 27. Manga enthusiasts can also delve into the SPY×FAMILY series in English through Viz Media’s publications and on Shueisha’s MANGA Plus service. Since September 2019, the manga has been accessible via Viz Media’s Shonen Jump subscription app, and print versions are also available for avid collectors.

Summary







Muse Asia has recently unveiled exciting news for anime enthusiasts across Asia, as they announced the availability of the English dubbed version of the immensely popular "SPY×FAMILY" anime series. Adapted from the renowned manga by Tatsuya Endō, this highly anticipated streaming initiative will be accessible on Muse Asia's YouTube channel. The reach of this release extends to a total of 14 countries, including India, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, the Philippines, Singapore, Vietnam, and Pakistan. The journey of "SPY×FAMILY" in the realm of anime began with its premiere in Japan in April 2022, and its first cours were simultaneously made available to Indian fans on Muse Asia's YouTube channel and Netflix. The second cours followed suit on Netflix in October 2022, solidifying the anime's success.

Fans have even more to look forward to, with a second season of the anime scheduled for release on October 7 and a special film titled "Gekijōban SPY×FAMILY Code: White," featuring an original storyline, captivating audiences on December 22. In India, Crunchyroll started streaming the English dub on February 1, but Muse Asia had already treated fans to it on January 27. Manga enthusiasts can also delve into the "SPY×FAMILY" series in English through Viz Media's publications and on Shueisha's MANGA Plus service. The manga has been available on Viz Media's Shonen Jump subscription app since September 2019, with print versions catering to avid collectors. In summary, Muse Asia's announcement of the English dubbed "SPY×FAMILY" anime's availability in India and 13 additional Asian countries is a significant development for fans, expanding the reach of this beloved series and offering a delightful array of content to look forward to in the coming months.
